About

UNIKALA studio is a multidisciplinary architecture and interior design firm based in Pune, India.

We make an effort to design stunning environments that are both practical and aesthetically pleasant while also being conscious of our influence on the environment.

We understand that designing a space is an highly personal process, so we make sure that you feel comfortable throughout the entire designing journey.
